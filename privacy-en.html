Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Abdallah Alshemeri ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Amwali mobile application ("App").

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and financial information. This policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Apple ID (CloudKit) Cloud synchronization Apple's CloudKit
Device Information App functionality and support Local device only
Usage Analytics App improvement Anonymized, local processing
Crash Reports Bug fixes and stability Apple's crash reporting service

1.2 Financial Information

Amwali stores your financial data locally on your device and optionally in Apple's CloudKit:

  • Transaction Records: Amount, date, category, description
  • Account Balances: Current balances for your accounts
  • Budget Information: Monthly budgets and spending limits
  • Trip Expenses: Travel-related financial planning data
  • Calculator Results: Zakat and loan calculation results
Important: We do not collect or store actual bank account numbers, credit card details, or other sensitive financial credentials. All financial data is user-entered and remains under your control.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • App version and device model
  • Operating system version
  • App usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • App Functionality: Provide core financial management features
  • Data Synchronization: Sync your data across your Apple devices
  • Calculations: Perform financial calculations (Zakat, loans, etc.)
  • Reports: Generate financial reports and analytics
  • Backup: Maintain secure backups of your data

2.2 Secondary Uses

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Develop new features based on usage patterns
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Primary Storage: All your financial data is primarily stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework with encryption.

3.2 Cloud Storage

If you enable cloud synchronization:

  • Data is stored in Apple's CloudKit service
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Accessible only through your Apple ID
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policies

3.3 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Access Control: Data access is limited to necessary app functions
  • No Third-Party Access: We do not share your data with third parties
  • Secure Transmission: All network communications use HTTPS/TLS
  • Local Authentication: Support for Face ID, Touch ID, and passcode protection

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information to third parties.

4.1 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Safety: To protect the safety of users or the public
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist in app operation (Apple services)

4.2 Apple Services

We use the following Apple services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • CloudKit: For data synchronization
  • StoreKit: For subscription management
  • App Store Connect: For analytics and crash reporting

5. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Control

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modify: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Delete: Remove specific transactions or all data
  • Export: Export your data for personal use
  • Cloud Sync: Enable or disable cloud synchronization

5.2 Account Management

  • Manage subscription through App Store settings
  • Delete app and all local data by uninstalling
  • Disable CloudKit sync in iOS Settings
  • Contact us for data deletion requests

6. Data Retention

6.1 Local Data

Data stored locally on your device remains until you:

  • Delete specific transactions or categories
  • Reset the app data
  • Uninstall the application

6.2 Cloud Data

Data in CloudKit is retained according to:

  • Your CloudKit storage settings
  • Apple's data retention policies
  • Your explicit deletion requests

7. International Data Transfers

If you use Amwali outside your home country:

  • Data may be processed in different countries
  • We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place
  • Apple's global infrastructure provides data protection
  • Your data receives the same level of protection

8. Children's Privacy

Age Restriction: Amwali is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Amwali does not use:

  • Web cookies (as it's a native app)
  • Third-party tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media plugins

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will notify you through the app
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Significant changes will be highlighted
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance

12. Data Breach Notification

  • We will investigate immediately
  • Affected users will be notified promptly
  • Authorities will be notified as required by law
  • We will provide guidance on protective measures

13. Your Consent

By using Amwali, you consent to:

  • This Privacy Policy
  • Collection and use of information as described
  • Data processing necessary for app functionality
  • Storage of data as outlined in this policy
